A few words about Match-N-Neet

Project significance:

We have noticed that our university offers less desired events and meetings, than some other universities. Events would be a good opportunity for students to meet and take a break from work. Because of this problem, a certain event application could be useful, that can prioritize events that are more favourable by student. Such app could collect data and surveys from the students and provide statistics to the organization in order to create more relevant and wanted events.

The problem:

Our university lack meeting events between friends and students. Our application (Match-N-Meet) will solve this problem by organizing events and matching the student personality to other students and other events. Our solution is to provide a questionnaire for the students, and with the help of our algorithm, will match them to corresponding friends and events, and provide statistics to the organization itself to better choose its events .
